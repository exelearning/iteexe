About eXeLearning

eXeLearning

  • eLearning XHTML editor

eXe is an Open Source authoring application to assist teachers and academics in the publishing of web content.

It grew out of the New Zealand Government Tertiary Education Commission's eCollaboration Fund and was led by the University of Auckland, The Auckland University of Technology, and Tairawhiti Polytechnic. It was later supported by CORE Education.

eXe is currently supported by the Spanish Government and other institutions and companies and assisted by a global group of participants and contributors.

See "Current version" and "Previous versions" for more information.
